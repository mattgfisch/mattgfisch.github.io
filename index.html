What makes site good/bad?

  • I like the nav bar. It looks nice and the information is organized well underneath each tab. Makes it easy to navigate around the site.
  • I like how each section is separated by a thin black bar at the top, with the title on the left and content in middle/right. It's intuitive, but also a distinct way to organize content.
  • I don't like how you don't know what is on the home page until you scroll down. It would be nice to have some kind of menu for the home page, or make the sections smaller and slimmed down in terms of content. That way you can be intrigued by a piece of content on the home page, but dive into the full version of the section by clicking on a link. It would make the home page look less cluttered.
